# UK 本硕 Marking Rubric（通用商科版）

UK 评分有明文分档，每档对应**质的差异**而不是量的。复习/答题时永远对照这张表：**你的目标是哪一档？这一段对应这一档的哪个标准？**

---

## 分档对照表

| 等级 | 分数 | 关键词 |
|---|---|---|
| **1st (First)** | 70-100 | Original, critical, sophisticated, well-evidenced |
| **2:1 (Upper Second)** | 60-69 | Solid, well-structured, accurate, some critique |
| **2:2 (Lower Second)** | 50-59 | Descriptive, mostly accurate, limited critique |
| **3rd (Third)** | 40-49 | Basic understanding, errors present |
| **Fail** | <40 | Misunderstanding or off-topic |

**硕士对应：Distinction (70+) / Merit (60-69) / Pass (50-59) / Fail (<50)。**

---

## 各档详细标准（对答题最有用的版本）

### 1st (70+) —— 拿到这档的本质：你 OWN 这个 topic

- **Critical engagement**：不止讲框架，还讨论框架的 limitation / 反例 / boundary condition
- **Synthesis**：能把 2+ 个理论联系起来比较，不是孤立讲
- **Original argument**：有自己的 thesis（不是简单选边），且有理有据
- **Evidence rich**：每个 claim 都有 citation + empirical study
- **Academic writing**：sophisticated language，hedging 得当，signposting 清晰

**对答题的指令：** 在每段后加 "However" / "On the other hand" / "Critics argue..."

### 2:1 (60-69) —— 大部分认真学生的天花板

- **Structured & accurate**：内容正确、结构清晰
- **Some critique**：会提一两个 limitation，但不深入
- **Good use of evidence**：有 citation，但来源单一
- **Clear writing**：表达清楚但不 sophisticated

**对答题的指令：** 至少在 conclusion 处提一个 "However" 或 limitation。

### 2:2 (50-59) —— 复述型答案的上限

- **Descriptive**：基本是 lecture slide 的复述
- **Accurate but shallow**：内容对但没深度
- **Minimal critique**：几乎不批判
- **Limited evidence**：citation 少或不规范

**警示：** 如果你的草稿读起来像 "X is..., X consists of..., X is important because..."，全是陈述句，这就是 2:2 答案。**加 "However" + "Critics argue" 立刻升档**。

### 3rd (40-49) 和 Fail

- 概念错误、跑题、citation 完全缺失。**复习时不用对照这两档。**

---

## 答题前 60 秒自查清单（每道 Essay 题用）

1. **我的 thesis 是什么？**（一句话总结我的立场）
2. **我有几个 supporting points？**（至少 3 个，每个有 evidence）
3. **我有 counterargument 吗？**（至少 1 个，并 rebuttal）
4. **我每个 claim 都有 citation 吗？**（作者 + 年份）
5. **我的 conclusion 不只是重复 intro 吗？**（应该有 "However, this view has limitations..."）

5 条全过 = 至少 2:1。前 4 条 + 第 5 条做到 synthesis = 冲 1st。

---

## Signposting 词库（让答案读起来 "academic"）

**Introducing point：** Firstly, Moreover, Furthermore, In addition  
**Contrasting：** However, On the other hand, Conversely, In contrast, Nevertheless  
**Evidence：** This is supported by..., A study by X (year) found...  
**Critique：** Critics argue..., This view has been challenged by..., A key limitation is...  
**Hedging（一定要用！）：** tends to, may suggest, appears to, is likely to, is generally regarded as  
**Synthesis：** Taken together, On balance, While X is useful in Y context, it has limitations in Z  
**Conclusion：** In conclusion, Overall, This essay has argued that...
