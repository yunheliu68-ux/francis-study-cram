# 英式拼写 + 学术英语速查

UK 评分会因 American spelling 扣印象分（不致命但累积起来明显）。Skill 写英文答案时**强制英式拼写**。

**ize / ise 规则**：UK 学界两种都接受（Oxford 用 -ize），但 -ise 更安全、更"英式感"。**统一用 -ise**。

## 引用规范（Harvard 风格 —— UK 商科最常用）

**In-text citation：**
- 单一作者：`(Smith, 2020)` 或 `Smith (2020) argues that...`
- 两位作者：`(Smith & Jones, 2020)`
- 三位以上：`(Smith et al., 2020)`
- 直接引用必须带页码：`(Smith, 2020, p.45)`

**Reference list 格式：**
```
Smith, J. (2020) Title of Book. London: Publisher.
Smith, J. and Jones, A. (2021) 'Article title', Journal Name, 15(3), pp.45-67.
```

**OSCOLA**（法律专用）—— SMO207 这类法律模块用 OSCOLA，**不是 Harvard**：
- Case：`Donoghue v Stevenson [1932] AC 562`
- Statute：`Employment Rights Act 1996, s 94`
- Article：`J Smith, 'Title' (2020) 15 Modern Law Review 45`

## Hedging 必备短语（拿 1st 的关键）

学术写作几乎不写绝对句。用 hedging 表示 "我知道这是 nuanced"：

- **可能性 hedging**：may, might, could, tends to, appears to, seems to, is likely to
- **频率 hedging**：often, generally, typically, in most cases, frequently
- **量级 hedging**：a degree of, somewhat, to some extent, partially
- **来源 hedging**：it has been suggested that, evidence indicates, research suggests

**例：**
- ❌ "Globalisation causes inequality."
- ✅ "Globalisation may contribute to inequality, particularly in developing economies."

## 必备 academic 短语库

**开头：**
- This essay will argue that...
- The purpose of this essay is to...
- This essay critically examines...

**Transition：**
- In light of the above...
- Building on this...
- This raises the question of...

**Critique：**
- A key limitation of this approach is...
- This view has been challenged on the grounds that...
- However, this argument overlooks...

**Synthesis：**
- Taking these perspectives together...
- On balance, the evidence suggests...
- While both views have merit, X is more persuasive because...

**Conclusion：**
- In conclusion, this essay has argued that...
- The evidence presented suggests that...
- Further research is needed to determine...
